How can you help..?

Yes,even you can help too..

We talked about many steps that can be taken to prevent farmers suicide's but, those steps require large scale implementation and requires lot of capital investment. But as an induvidual what can you do?. There are lot of things that you can help in you local.If you are living in rural india or small towns you will easily know about your local farmers. Reach out to them find out problems that they are facing if possible try to help in your way like if they are facing financial problems try to give some financial advice and educate them to take loans from banks where they offer low intrest rate rather than the local lender.

If the farmer you know is illiterate and does'nt know how to bargain with middlemen for right price for their crops then help them in educating about the present market rate they should get for their harvest , so that they can have their right to bargain.

In some reports it also came to my notice that that lot of farmers commit suicide due to getting cheated by fake CHIT FUNDS.Try and warn them about high intrest promising chit funds, most of the time those type of chit funds will be a scam.

Chronic illness are also play a vital role in pushing farmers to death bed.As you know illness only farmers does'nt get illness everybody gets them, but the difference is educated and urban communities have awareness of treating harmful dieseases, but rural india as of my knowledge many people does'nt care about them especialy poor farmers. so try educating them about harmful diesease and their effect and also helping them in getting benifits from many of govt schemes.

Even today in rural areas lot of family practice dowry systems. And many farmers who are not capable of giving dowry end up hanging themselves. If you find about such practices happening in your local try informing Police about such acts, gradually this will help eradicating such practice in your local, so many lives could be saved.
